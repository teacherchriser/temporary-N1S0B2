In Spain, music is really important. People usually listen to reggaeton, trap, and pop, while traditional rhythms like flamenco still influence modern music. Artists like Rosalía and Aitana are very popular, and traditional guitars are commonly used. In the 20th century, the “Movida Madrileña” changed Spanish culture after the dictatorship ended, and bands like 'Mecano' became very famous.

Nowadays, many young listeners are mixing flamenco with modern beats on Spotify and YouTube. One important artist is C. Tangana, who mixes traditional music with modern rap. Right now, he is showing Spanish culture to the world in a modern way.

On the other hand, Finland has a very unique music scene. It has more heavy metal bands than any other country. People listen to metal, pop, and rap, but traditional rhythms and instruments like the Kantele are still important today. In the 1990s, Finnish metal became famous worldwide with bands like Nightwish.

Today, many young people in Finland are listening to rap in their own language. Producers are experimenting with natural sounds like forests and ice to create new styles. A very popular artist is Käärijä, who combines metal, rap, and electronic music. After Eurovision, he is becoming more famous every day.
